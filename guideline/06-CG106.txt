## Diagnosis
* All treatments for high-grade dysplasia and intramucosal cancer in Barrett's oesophagus should be performed by specialist oesophago-gastric cancer teams

## Investigation* n/a

## Management* Endoscopic therapy i.e. circumferential endoscopic mucosal resection as an alternate to oesophagectomy with high grade (T1a) dysplasia if unsuitable for surgery or do not wish to undergo oesophagectomy* If residual or recurrent disease offer either endoscopic mucosal resection and / or ablative therapy to completely remove residual flat dysplasia
