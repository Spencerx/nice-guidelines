# Generalised Anxiety, Social Anxiety Disorder, OCD and PTSD

## Diagnosis
* n/a
  
## Investigation

* Assess severity:
	* Social anxiety: Mini social phobia inventory
	  
## Management

### Acute
* Respiratory alkalosis
* Self-harm and suicide risk
  
### Conservative
* Social anxiety: 1st line individual CBT (not group)
* OCD: local self help and support groups; CBT including Exposure Response Prevention (ERP)
* PTSD: <3mths Watchful waiting; Relaxation >3mths focused CBT / eye movement desensitisation
* Phobia: Behavioural therapy – non collaborative stage-wise desensitization (eg spider)
  
###  Medical
* Social / OCD / Panic: 2nd Line SSRI (Sertraline). Monitor 1st week, increased suicide risk. Relapse risk unlike CBT
* PTSD: 2nd line NSRI Mirtazepine (specialists only)
* Severe cases: Intensive CBT + SSRI
* Anxiety 20 to depression, treating depression first
