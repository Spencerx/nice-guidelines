# Alcohol Mis-use Disorder

## Diagnosis
* n/a
  
## Investigation

* Physical Examination: signs of liver disease
* Exclude other sources of liver disease: LFTs
* Quantify: FBC for MCV (macrocytic); GGT
* Liver Biopsy to confirm hepatitis
* Imagining: CT pancreas

## Management

### Acute
* Withdrawal: Benzo (Chlordiazepoxide)
* Delerium Tremens: Lorazepam (quick acting)
* Wernicke’s: Thiamine B1 (Pabrinex IV)
  
### Conservative
* Councilling (alcoholics Anonymous), but social grouping may lead to drinking
* Cognitive Behavioural Therapy relapse prevent
* Motivational interviewing
  
### Medical
* Disulfiram (Antabuse), blocks acetaldehyde
* Naltrexone: remove reinforcing action
* Acamprocate: reduce desire
  
### Surgical
* Liver tx: if decomp CLD
